About the Priory

A Home for Dominican Life in Columbus

St. Patrick's Priory is home to the Dominican Friars who serve St. Patrick Church in the heart of downtown Columbus, Ohio. Rooted in the eight-hundred-year tradition of the Order of Preachers founded by St. Dominic de Guzmán, the friars live a common life of prayer, study, and preaching.


The priory is a place of fraternity, contemplation, and mission — where the friars are formed and sustained for their apostolate of bringing the Gospel to the world.

Preaching & Ministry

Bringing the Gospel to the World

As sons of St. Dominic, our charism is preaching. From the pulpit to the classroom, from retreats to the streets, we proclaim the truth of Jesus Christ for the salvation of souls.

01

Liturgical Preaching

The Sunday homily is the heart of Dominican preaching. Our friars prepare each homily through deep study of Sacred Scripture and the living Tradition of the Church.

02

Teaching & Formation

From RCIA to adult faith formation, Bible studies to lecture series, the friars provide robust intellectual formation rooted in the thought of St. Thomas Aquinas.

03

Retreats & Missions

Our friars are available for parish missions, days of recollection, retreats, and conferences — bringing the Dominican tradition of preaching to communities near and far.
