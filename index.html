About the Priory

A Home for Dominican Life in Columbus

St. Patrick's Priory is home to the Dominican Friars who serve St. Patrick Church in the heart of downtown Columbus, Ohio. Rooted in the eight-hundred-year tradition of the Order of Preachers founded by St. Dominic de Guzmán, the friars live a common life of prayer, study, and preaching.

Preaching & Ministry

Bringing the Gospel to the World

As sons of St. Dominic, our charism is preaching. From the pulpit to the classroom, from retreats to the streets, we proclaim the truth of Jesus Christ.

01

Liturgical Preaching

The Sunday homily is the heart of Dominican preaching, prepared through deep study of Sacred Scripture and Tradition.

02

Teaching & Formation

From RCIA to adult faith formation, the friars provide robust intellectual formation rooted in St. Thomas Aquinas.

03

Retreats & Missions

Our friars are available for parish missions, retreats, and conferences throughout the Province.
