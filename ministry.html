Preaching & Ministry

Bringing the Gospel to the World

As sons of St. Dominic, our charism is preaching — from the pulpit to the streets.

01 — Preaching

Liturgical Preaching

The Sunday homily is the heart of Dominican preaching. Our friars prepare each homily through deep study of Sacred Scripture, the Church Fathers, and the living Tradition of the Church. Dominican preaching aims not merely to inform but to transform — to set hearts on fire with the love of God.


Beyond the Sunday Mass, the friars preach at daily Masses, holy hours, novenas, and special liturgical celebrations throughout the year.

02 — Formation

Teaching & Formation

From RCIA to adult faith formation, Bible studies to lecture series, the friars provide robust intellectual formation rooted in the thought of St. Thomas Aquinas. The Dominican intellectual tradition holds that faith and reason are complementary paths to truth.


Current programs include a weekly Aquinas study circle, seasonal lecture series on the Catechism, and a thriving RCIA program welcoming those exploring the Catholic faith.

03 — Outreach

Retreats & Missions

Our friars are available for parish missions, days of recollection, retreats, and conferences — bringing the Dominican tradition of preaching to communities near and far. We welcome invitations from parishes, schools, and organizations throughout the diocese and beyond.
